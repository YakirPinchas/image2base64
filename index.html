Online Image to Base64 & Base64 to Image Converter

This free tool lets you quickly convert one or multiple images to Base64 strings, or paste an existing Base64 value and preview the image in your browser. All conversions run locally in your browser using JavaScript ‚Äì your files are never uploaded or stored on any server.

Common use cases:
